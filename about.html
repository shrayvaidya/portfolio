About Me

I am a third year student at Northwestern University studying Computer Science with minors in Data Science, Classics, and South Asian Humanities. In my free time, I like to read, play videogames, work out, and try out new recipes. Outside of academics, I am also the Treasurer for the South Asian Students Alliance as well ac the C40 Committee Chair for Northwestern Model United Nations.

My professional experience includes two summers interning at the cybersecurity firm IronSDN Corp., where I worked as a Software Development/Data Science Intern. In this role, I worked directly on training and testing AI-based systems to detect the breaking of glass as well as facial recognition. Other projects were focused on web development, where I applied my skills in HTML, CSS, and Javascript (React) to build sites to train clients on common phishing tactics. I collected and analyzed over 500,000 data points from client systems on their readiness to defend against cyber threats using SQL, Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-Learn) and built a visualization dashboard with Tableau. Outside of these internships, I also work part time at Northwestern McCormick IT as a Computer Aide, where I am responsible for maintaining several computer labs and troubleshooting various software and hardware issues.

Currently, I am seeking internships for Summer 2023 in Software Engineering, Data Analytics, Data Engineering, AI/Machine Learning, and other technology-related fields. Please reach out if interested!
